Tbilisi & Beyond

5 Days / 4 Nights · From $650

Overview

Discover the charm of Georgia’s capital and the serenity of Mtskheta in a perfectly curated 5-day escape. Wander through cobbled streets, explore ancient monasteries, and savor local cuisine and wines.

  • 4-star hotel stays with daily breakfast
  • Airport transfers & comfortable transport
  • Professional English-speaking guide
  • Wine tasting experience in Old Tbilisi

What's Included

Itinerary

1
Day 1: Arrival in Tbilisi

• Afternoon – Arrive at Tbilisi International Airport
• Private transfer to hotel and check-in
• Welcome drink and orientation with your local guide
• 7:00 PM – Welcome dinner at traditional Georgian restaurant with wine tasting
• Overnight in Tbilisi

2
Day 2: Old Tbilisi Exploration

• 9:00 AM – Breakfast at hotel
• 10:00 AM – Ride the cable car to Narikala Fortress
• 11:30 AM – Visit Sulfur Baths, Leghvtakhevi Waterfall
• 1:00 PM – Lunch at a local restaurant in Old Town
• 2:30 PM – Visit Metekhi Church & stroll in Rike Park
• 4:00 PM – Optional visit to Dry Bridge Market or café hopping
• Evening at leisure, overnight in Tbilisi

3
Day 3: Mtskheta Day Trip

• 8:30 AM – Breakfast and depart to Mtskheta (30 min drive)
• 9:30 AM – Visit Jvari Monastery with panoramic views
• 11:00 AM – Explore Svetitskhoveli Cathedral
• 1:00 PM – Lunch overlooking Aragvi and Mtkvari rivers
• 2:30 PM – Stop for local souvenir shopping
• 4:00 PM – Return to Tbilisi and evening at leisure

4
Day 4: Free Day & Optional Activities

• Morning – Breakfast at your own pace
• Optional Experiences:
  – Georgian cooking class
  – Wine blending workshop
  – Guided art walk or photo tour
• Afternoon – Free time to shop, relax, or revisit favorite spots
• Evening – Optional farewell dinner at elevated viewpoint restaurant

5
Day 5: Departure

• 8:00 AM – Breakfast at hotel
• Private transfer to airport according to flight schedule
• Assistance with luggage and final farewell by local team
